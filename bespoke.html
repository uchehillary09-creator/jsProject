The Journey

Our Process

A meticulous, personal journey from the first hello to your final look.

01

Private Consultation

We sit and talk — your occasion, your style, your vision. No templates. Pure conversation that becomes your blueprint.

02

Measurements & Pattern

Over 25 body measurements taken by hand. Your personal pattern is drafted from scratch — not from a block.

03

Fabric Selection

Browse our curated library of imported and local premium fabrics. We guide you on drape, weight, and finish.

04

Toile Fitting

A muslin mock-up is made and fitted on you first. We refine before touching your actual fabric — perfection, not assumption.

05

Construction

Your garment is hand-built by our expert tailors. Every seam, lining, and finish executed with precision and care.

06

Final Fitting & Handover

You try it on. We make any last-minute tweaks on the spot. Then it's yours — pressed, boxed, and absolutely perfect.

Materials

Our Fabric Library

Every bespoke piece draws from our curated selection of premium fabrics sourced locally and internationally.

LIN

Premium Linen

Breathable, structured, and effortlessly elegant. Ideal for warm-weather tailoring.

SAT

Duchess Satin

Rich and heavy with a matte sheen. Drapes beautifully for gowns and formal wear.

WOL

Italian Wool

Soft, structured, and luxuriously warm. The gold standard for suiting.

CHF

French Chiffon

Ethereal and floaty — perfect for overlays and dramatic silhouettes.

SLK

Silk Charmeuse

Liquid drape and luminous sheen. The ultimate evening wear fabric.

ASO

Aso-Oke & Aso-Ebi

Heritage fabrics woven into contemporary silhouettes with pride.

VEL

Velvet

Opulent texture for bold evening looks. Multiple weights and finishes available.

POP

Cotton Poplin

Crisp and clean — the perfect canvas for structured day pieces.
