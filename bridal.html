Planning Guide

When to Start

Bridal couture takes time. Start early and your experience will be seamless, beautiful, and stress-free.

12

12 Months Before - Ideal

Start now. Maximum time to refine the design, source special fabrics, and take your time through fittings with zero pressure.

9

9 Months Before - Comfortable

Still very comfortable. Construction begins, fabric ordered. Plenty of time for multiple fitting sessions.

6

6 Months Before - Minimum Recommended

Manageable for most styles. We can accommodate 6-month timelines. Rush fees may apply for complex embellishments.

3

3 Months - Rush Service

For simpler silhouettes we offer rush service. Contact us immediately to check availability - slots are limited.
