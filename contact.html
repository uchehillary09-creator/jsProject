Common Questions

FAQ

Quick answers before you reach out - though we're always happy to go deeper.

How long does a bespoke garment take?
+

Most bespoke pieces take 4-8 weeks from consultation to handover, depending on complexity. Bridal garments need a minimum of 6 months (12 recommended). Rush service is available for some pieces.

What is your pricing range?
+

Ready-to-wear starts from ₦75,000. Bespoke starts from ₦150,000. Bridal couture starts from ₦280,000. We provide a full itemised quote before any work begins - no surprises.

Do you ship nationally?
+

Yes. We ship ready-to-wear pieces across Nigeria via insured courier. For bespoke and bridal items, we require in-person fitting sessions, but we can discuss remote options on request.

Can I bring my own fabric?
+

Absolutely. We welcome heritage fabrics, gifted silks, and personal materials. We'll assess suitability in your consultation and advise on the best design approach.

Do you design for both men and women?
+

Yes - Neyduh's Fashion Hub proudly serves both men and women. Our RTW, bespoke, and bridal services are fully available to everyone with equal attention and care.

Where is the atelier located?
+

We operate by appointment only. Our full address is shared with confirmed clients upon booking - this keeps every visit private, personal, and entirely focused on you.
