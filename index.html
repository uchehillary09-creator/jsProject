Atelier Experience

From First Consultation to Final Reveal

The Neyduh process is calm, hands-on, and deeply considered. We guide fit, proportion, fabrication, and finishing so the final garment feels effortless on the body and unmistakable in the room.

  • Private design consultation: we begin with the occasion, mood, body language, and the image you want to leave behind.
  • Fabric and silhouette direction: we help you choose drape, weight, finish, and detailing that match your exact moment.
  • Fittings and refinements: nothing is rushed. Every adjustment is made until the piece carries the right presence.
