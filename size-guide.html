Our Size Guide

Use this as a starting point for ready-to-wear. If you want a closer fit, unusual proportions, or a special-event finish, bespoke is the better route.

Ready-to-Wear Fit

Our ready-to-wear pieces are designed with a refined, flattering silhouette. If you sit between sizes, we usually recommend sizing up for tailoring flexibility.

  • XS-S for a closer, sharper fit.
  • M-L for a balanced classic fit.
  • XL and above when comfort, drape, or layering matter more.

What To Measure

For the most accurate direction, share your bust or chest, waist, hips, and height. For suiting, shoulder width is especially helpful.

  • Measure over light clothing.
  • Keep the tape level and comfortably snug.
  • Round to the nearest half inch or centimetre.

When To Choose Bespoke

Bespoke is ideal for bridal, major events, strong fit preferences, traditional fabrics, or whenever you want a garment built around you from the start.
